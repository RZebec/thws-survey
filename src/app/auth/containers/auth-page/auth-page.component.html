Welcome to our Survey!

We are developing a new concept for our task management app by integrating tutorials directly into the app as interactive todo lists.

Learning how to use a new app can be overwhelming, which is why we're creating a seamless learning experience. By integrating the tutorial as a todo list itself, we aim to make it easier and more engaging for you to get started.

Your feedback is essential as we refine this concept. Rest assured that your responses will be kept confidential and anonymized according to our privacy policy.

Thank you for joining us on this journey to explore new possibilities for app tutorials. Let's begin the survey and shape the future of our app together.
